## Brand & Style
The design system embodies a sophisticated "Mocha Glass" aesthetic, merging the precision of modern minimalism with the tactile richness of skeuomorphic glassmorphism.

## Elevation & Depth
1. **Base Surface:** Solid Cream floor (#F5F5F0) with subtle bronze grain.
2. **Floating Glass:** Semi-transparent (80-90% opacity) with backdrop-filter: blur(20px). 1px inner-border highlight.
3. **Aura Shadows:** Diffused dropshadows tinted with deep amber/bronze (#A45D3D), high blur (30px+), low opacity (15%).

## Components
- **Buttons:** Primary = solid Espresso (#3E2723) with bronze inner-glow. Secondary = translucent blur + Cedar border.
- **Cards:** Signature "Aura" shadow. 1px top-left highlight + 1px bottom-right shadow-stroke for 3D refractive effect.
- **Input Fields:** Recessed "Clay" backgrounds with soft inner shadow ("pressed" into sand surface).
- **Chips:** Pill-shaped, subtle amber tint, 40% opacity.
- **Refractive Dividers:** 1px gradient line: transparent → Bronze (#A45D3D) → transparent.
